Explore the Latest Government Schemes

Discover the various schemes launched by the Government of India to support farmers and agricultural development.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Provides insurance coverage and financial support to farmers suffering from crop failure due to natural calamities.

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

KCC offers farmers access to credit at affordable rates for crop production and related activities.

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

This scheme promotes organic farming, helping farmers to use eco-friendly and sustainable farming methods.

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY)

Aims to improve water-use efficiency and expand the area under irrigation to ensure water to every farm.

National Agriculture Market (eNAM)

eNAM is an online trading platform for farmers to sell their produce at competitive prices across the country.

Soil Health Card Scheme

This scheme helps farmers analyze the quality of their soil and recommends the appropriate nutrients for better yield.

Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY)

RKVY encourages growth in the agricultural sector by providing funds for state-specific agricultural plans.
